Hi, I'm Pat, a PhD candidate in Sociology at the University of California, Berkeley.

I primarily study how economic inequality shapes attitudes and behaviors. More generally, I use surveys, experiments, and administrative data to do research in stratification, inequality, economic sociology, social psychology, demography, culture, religion & spirituality, and quantitative methods.

To learn more about my dissertation and other projects, visit my research page or look at my CV [pdf]. You can also read a bit more about me or check out my teaching page.

If you're interested in talking about my work, or yours, get in touch!
