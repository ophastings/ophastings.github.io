Hi, I'm Pat, a PhD candidate in Sociology at the University of California, Berkeley. In Fall 2017, I will join Colorado State University as an Assistant Professor of Sociology.

I use surveys, experiments, and administrative data to do research in stratification, inequality, economic sociology, social psychology, social demography, religion & spirituality, and quantitative methods. I am especially interested in how economic inequality shapes attitudes and behaviors.

View my research page or CV [pdf] to learn more about my dissertation and other projects. You can also read a bit more about me or check out my teaching page. If you're interested in talking about my work, or yours, get in touch!
