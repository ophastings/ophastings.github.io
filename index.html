Hi, I'm Pat, a PhD candidate in Sociology at the University of California, Berkeley.

I am currently exploring how attitudes and behaviors are shaped by economic inequality. More generally, I am interested in using surveys, experiments, and administrative data to study stratification, inequality, economic sociology, social psychology, demography, culture, religion & spirituality, and social change.

To learn more about my current and past projects, visit my research page or look at my CV [pdf]. You can also read a bit more about me or check out my teaching page.

If you're interested in talking about my work, or yours, get in touch!
