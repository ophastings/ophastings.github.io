Hi, I'm Pat, a PhD candidate in Sociology at the University of California, Berkeley.

My research explores how people's attitudes and behaviors are shaped by the economic contextual factors (e.g., income inequality or unemployment) they live in. I am more generally interested in using surveys and administrative data to study stratification, inequality, culture, religion, and organizations.

To learn more about my current and past projects, visit my research page. Or you can read a bit more about me or some of my favorite resources.
