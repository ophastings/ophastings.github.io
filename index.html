Hi, I'm Pat, a PhD candidate in Sociology at the University of California, Berkeley.

I am currently exploring how attitudes and behaviors are shaped by contextual economic factors such as income inequality, average income, and unemployment. More generally, I am interested in using surveys, experiments, and administrative data to study stratification, inequality, social change, economy & society, culture, and religion.

To learn more about my current and past projects, visit my research page. Or you can read a bit more about me or some of my favorite resources.
